How it works

Four steps.
Real evidence.

01

Pick a skill

Add any skill you want to build — reading, coding, gym, music. Set your goal and how often you plan to show up each week.

02

Log every session

After each session, answer three questions: what you did, what you learned, what you created. Upload proof if you have it — photos, PDFs, docs.

03

Get instant AI feedback

AI reads your log and proof immediately. It acknowledges what you did, spots growth patterns from your past sessions, and asks one coaching question to push you deeper.

04

Watch yourself level up

After 30 days, Levlr generates a full review. See who you were on Day 1 versus who you are now. The proof is in your own logs.
